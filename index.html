Mental Health

Mental health should not just be an issue, but also a priority

Anxiety Facts

  • There are many type of anxiety, called anxiety disorders. One example is PTSD (Post Traumatic Stess Disorder)

  • It is normal to have feelings of anxiety, it is your body's natural response to stress.

  • On an average in Australia, one in three women experiences and one in five men will have experience anxiety in their life

Treatments

Some ways to cure anxiety

  • Exercise. Exercising eases anxiety, improving physical and mental health.

  • Ditch caffeine. Caffeine is known to cause jitters, nervousness, making anxiety worse

  • Meditation. Meditating will make your mind clear up, removing any worries

  • Talk to someone. By doing this you can express your feelings into words

If you need further help with anxiety, visit https://www.headtohealth.gov.au/crisis-links. It has all the help you need.

Symptoms

  • Feeling nervous or tense

  • Increased heart rate

  • Hyperventilation

  • Feeling weak and tired all the time

  • Hard time concentrating

  • Troubles with sleeping

  • Feeling weird in stomach

Causes

  • Having multiple important things to do

  • Job interview

  • Performing in front of people

  • Family problems

  • Financial issues

Note

People get anxiety from stress. Take note that anxiety may not be caused by one issues, but multiple issues.
